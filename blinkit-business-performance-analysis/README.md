# Blinkit Business Performance Analysis

## Business Context

Blinkit operates across multiple outlet types, sizes, and geographic tiers.
Understanding which factors drive sales performance is critical for improving revenue and operational efficiency.

This project analyzes sales data to identify revenue drivers, outlet performance differences, and product-level contributions.

---

## Objectives

* Evaluate overall business performance using key KPIs
* Identify top revenue-driving product categories
* Compare outlet performance across size, type, and location tier
* Analyze sales variation by outlet establishment year
* Extract actionable business insights from structured data

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales
* Average Sales per Item
* Total Items Sold
* Average Customer Rating

These KPIs provide a high-level overview of revenue generation and customer satisfaction.

## Key Insights

* Revenue is disproportionately concentrated in selected product categories.
* Outlet size and geographic tier significantly influence total sales.
* Sales performance varies by outlet establishment year, indicating maturity-related performance differences.
* Customer ratings suggest stable satisfaction across outlets.
